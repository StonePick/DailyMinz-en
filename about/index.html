Where Am I?

Welcome to DailyMinz, my personal blog. You can find many sorts of knowledge, reflections, observations and insights.

After a progressing duration of five or six months, we have now confirmed our main content. DailyMinz will provides readers with thinking and insights into the society, history and humanity, aiming to offer humble views.

Of course as I declared at the very inception of the blog, we are not confined to the realm of humanities. Instead, we dedicate to recording every step of my observations, learning and thinking, in order to leave a trace for my spiritual journey and sharing with you as mutual encouragement.

You can also at any time take part in the construction of this blog, providing your own thinking and opinions, inspiring those who come after.
